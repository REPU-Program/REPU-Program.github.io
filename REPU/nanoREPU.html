The nano Research Experience for Peruvian Undergrads (nanoREPU) is one of the programs within REPU. It is specifically designed for Peruvian undergraduate students seeking degrees in electrical engineering (EE), mechanical engineering (ME), physics (PHYS), material science (MS) or related fields at Peruvian institutions. These students should have a strong interest in attending graduate school and pursuing a career in research. 


Selected students will participate in a 3 to 6 months internship in which they will do intense research addressing fundamental problems in nanoelectronics and nanophysics of new 2D materials such as graphene, topological insulators and MoS2 at participating laboratories.  For more information, please send us your questions to nanorepu.admissions@gmail.com.


 

Funding


The nanoREPU program is free. However, it does not guarantee economic support for traveling or Room & Board. REPU will assist in identifying sources of funding. Additional support will be provided as funding becomes available. We would like to thank the peruvian institutions that have supported our first two nanoREPU students:

- Concytec
- Patronato UNI
- Universidad Nacional de Ingenieria
- IGI UNI
