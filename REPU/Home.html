Our goal is to support the development of scientific capacity in Peru by facilitating the training of the new generation of Peruvian scientist. In order to further this goal, REPU seeks to complement Peruvian undergraduate scientific education with a three to six-month research intensive internship in laboratories in the United States. Additionally, REPU encourages a strong connection among participants and motivates students to be active in the greater scientific community in Peru and abroad.


The REPU program is organized by postdoctoral fellows and graduate students at different universities who are interested in the advancement of science in Peru and Latin America.
