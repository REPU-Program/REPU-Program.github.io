Research Experience for Peruvian Undergraduates

 

REPU provides a unique opportunity that makes you redefine yourself as a scientist, your interests, what you are capable of doing, and the way you approach and think about science as well as your future goals and the role and type of contribution you would like to make to science in the long run.

Maria Jesus Olarte, REPU 2010

The Research Experience for Peruvian Undergrads (REPU)  is designed for Peruvian undergraduate students seeking degrees in biology, genetics, biochemistry, biophysics or related fields at Peruvian institutions. These students should have a strong interest in attending graduate school and pursuing a career in biomedical research.


Selected students will participate in a 10-week internship in which they will do intense research addressing fundamental problems in biology and medicine at participating laboratories at Vanderbilt University in Nashville, TN and Yale University in New Haven, CT.


Students will...

Acquire intense laboratory research experience

Present and attend talks and journal clubs

Interact with the graduate school and university life

Discuss basic science and the development of science in Peru

Present a final oral and written presentation of research


Eligibility


3 years of completed undergraduate studies

Good English skills (oral and written)

Experience in basic laboratory skills

Oral and written presentation of research

Good standing at a Peruvian University 


Funding


The REPU program is free. However, it does not guarantee economic support for traveling or Room & Board. REPU will assist in identifying sources of funding and housing. Additional support will be provided as funding becomes available.
